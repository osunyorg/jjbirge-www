To celebrate its 40th anniversary, the Italian record label ADN commissioned a piece from 40 artists or groups of artists. They have assembled a  3 CD box set , issued in 250 hand-numbered copies with a 88 pp. booklet, on the left a presentation of the protagonists, on the right an image... It features the duet with Gwennaëlle Roulleau , for the piece Kakushi toride no san akunin , inspired by a photogram from Akira Kurosawa's film The Hidden Fortress .

To celebrate its 40th anniversary, the Italian record label ADN commissioned a piece from 40 artists or groups of artists. They have assembled a 3 CD box set, issued in 250 hand-numbered copies with a 88 pp. booklet, on the left a presentation of the protagonists, on the right an image... It features the duet with Gwennaëlle Roulleau (external link), for the piece Kakushi toride no san akunin, inspired by a photogram from Akira Kurosawa's film The Hidden Fortress.
