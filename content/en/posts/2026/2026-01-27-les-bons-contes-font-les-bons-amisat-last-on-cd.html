CD release of Les bons contes font les bons amis marks the end of the reissues of Un Drame Musical Instantané's LPs by the Austrian label Klang Galerie. If GRRR had released the first one, Trop d'adrénaline nuit , on CD, Walter Robotka's label had the following ones to its credit: Rideau ! (out of print), À travail égal salaire égal , L'homme à la caméra and Carnage . 15-piece orchestra. Beautiful cover by Jean Bruller, a.k.a. Vercors. N.B.: for LP lovers, Rideau ! , À travail égal salaire égal , Les bons contes font les bons amis , L'homme à la caméra are still available from GRRR (as well as from Les Allumés du Jazz, Orkhêstra and Souffle Continu) in their original pressing! On the other hand, LPs Trop d'adrénaline nuit and Carnage are sold out. Article on French Blog...

If GRRR had released the first one, Trop d'adrénaline nuit, on CD, Walter Robotka's label had the following ones to its credit: Rideau ! (out of print), À travail égal salaire égal, L'homme à la caméra and Carnage.
15-piece orchestra. Beautiful cover by Jean Bruller, a.k.a. Vercors.
N.B.: for LP lovers, Rideau !, À travail égal salaire égal, Les bons contes font les bons amis, L'homme à la caméra are still available from GRRR (as well as from Les Allumés du Jazz, Orkhêstra and Souffle Continu) in their original pressing! On the other hand, LPs Trop d'adrénaline nuit and Carnage are sold out.
