We are delighted to announce that the Charles Cros Academy has awarded
its Coup de Cœur prize in the 'recorded word' category to the album LES
DÉMENTS, featuring famous actor Denis Lavant , saxophonist Lionel Martin
and Jean-Jacques Birgé. It is a double CD on which Denis Lavant recites
texts by Marcel Moreau (1933-2020), André Martel (1893-1976), Xavier

Inouïe. It can also be listened to and purchased  on Bandcamp , and a clip can be viewed on Viméo .

OUCH!, Lionel's label, and GRRR, which is celebrating its 50th
anniversary this year. The artwork, featuring stains and eyes, was
created by Ella & Pitr .

We are delighted to announce that the Charles Cros Academy has awarded its Coup de Cœur prize in the 'recorded word' category to the album LES DÉMENTS, featuring famous actor Denis Lavant, saxophonist Lionel Martin and Jean-Jacques Birgé. It is a double CD on which Denis Lavant recites texts by Marcel Moreau (1933-2020), André Martel (1893-1976), Xavier Grall (1930-1981) and André Schlesser (1914-1985). It is distributed by Inouïe. It can also be listened to and purchased on Bandcamp, and a clip can be viewed on Viméo. Recorded and mixed by JJB at Studio GRRR, it is a co-production between OUCH!, Lionel's label, and GRRR, which is celebrating its 50th anniversary this year. The artwork, featuring stains and eyes, was created by Ella & Pitr.
