"An ode to life, a blend of sounds, the strength and beauty of words sublimated by the power and charisma of actor Denis Lavant in a trance with Madsaxx (Lionel Martin)'s saxophone to Jean-Jacques Birgé's unbridled, no-holds-barred music. A rock 'n roll liturgy!"
Following their LP Fictions inspired by Jose Luis Borges, released on the OUCH! label in 2022, Lionel Martin and Jean-Jacques Birgé invite Denis Lavant to join them for a day to improvise together on texts chosen by the actor. It was snowing that day in Paris.
