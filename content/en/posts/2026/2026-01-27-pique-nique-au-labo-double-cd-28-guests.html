Jean-Jacques BIRGÉ     Pique-Nique au Labo Double CD GRRR 2031-32 – dist. Orkhêstra 120 minutes of rare improvisations / unissued on CD


It's about playing to meet and not the other way around as usual. Most of the time the theme of each piece was randomly chosen just before playing.
