Following the "Coup de Cœur" award given last January to the album Les déments ,
it is a great honor to participate in the Académie Charles Cros Prix In
Honorem, awarded to Denis Lavant for his lifetime contribution to
poetry and poets, on the occasion of the publication of Fiches de Franz Kafka
(La Muse En Circuit) and the CD by the trio Les Déments featuring
saxophonist Lionel Martin and Jean-Jacques Birgé (a project they both
initiated and produced through their associated labels OUCH! and GRRR),
in the "Recorded Speech, Documents & Sound Creations" category.

Following the "Coup de Cœur" award given last January to the album Les déments, it is a great honor to participate in the Académie Charles Cros Prix In Honorem, awarded to Denis Lavant for his lifetime contribution to poetry and poets, on the occasion of the publication of Fiches de Franz Kafka (La Muse En Circuit) and the CD by the trio Les Déments featuring saxophonist Lionel Martin and Jean-Jacques Birgé (a project they both initiated and produced through their associated labels OUCH! and GRRR), in the "Recorded Speech, Documents & Sound Creations" category.
