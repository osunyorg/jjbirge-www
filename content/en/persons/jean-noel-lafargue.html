Jean-Noël Lafargue, b.1968, ungraduated training student of Paris fine art school, adjunct reader at Paris 8 University, arts professor in Le Havre art school, workshops in Pau, Angoulême, Rennes, Ensci. Trainingly teacher in Amiens arts & design school and e-artsup Paris. Blade english writer. Author or programr for a dozen of cd-roms. Not a real artist but doesn't use Very much his comb through. Teacher and programmer for pleasure, no serious job, then. Often works with artists such as Claude Closky and Jean-Louis Boissier. Author of the websiteScientists of Americaand of a few books about Processing Language (external link) (with Jean-Michel Geridan), comic book history (external link) and the End of the world (external link).
