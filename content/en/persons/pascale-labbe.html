Singer.
Pascale Labbé, born in 1957, is a vocalist, singer and composer.
In 1984 she created the association Nûba and then in 1990 the label Nûba with Jean Morières with which a form a duo.
She participated in several projects by Jean-Jacques Birgé (Sarajevo Next and end, Somnambules, Centenary of Jean-Jacques Birgé...).
