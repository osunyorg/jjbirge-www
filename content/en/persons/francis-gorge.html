First ever concert with Jean-Jacques Birgé in 1970.
He co-founded Un Drame Musical Instantané with Bernard Vitet and Jean Jacques Birgé in 1976, but left the group in 1992.
He continues his musical activities—composing and playing guitar—with, among others, the clowns Macloma and Pierre Debauche, the Celestial Communication Orchestra, singer Colette Magny, choreographers Mark Tompkins and Karine Saporta, Jacques Demarcq, Dominique Meens, and Jan Eerala.
He is currently recording a new album with Un Drame Musical Instantané alongside Jean-Jacques Birgé.
