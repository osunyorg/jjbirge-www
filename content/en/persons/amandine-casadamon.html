Sound artist and radio creator. His work is mostly based on real events or the fantastic slips into a documented concrete space.
Author and radio producer engaged on unexpected fronts, Amandine Casadamont is known for its radio programs often out-of-standard where the form is dictated by the subject. She doesn't hesitate to put herself in danger. to capture the invisible, and talk about fundamental and vital subjects as when travelling in a prohibited area in Fukushima or in the Mexican desert in drug trafficking zone. Winner many international awards such as the Prix du documentary sonore de the International Radio Biennale of Mexico City, the Phonurgia Art Prize radio sound, or the New York Festival Sound Art Award Category . Amandine is a polymorphic radio designer who can adapt to any type of program and format. Its productions in many fields (contemporary art, Mental Clubbing, cinema, for example), are known and can be heard in different countries.
