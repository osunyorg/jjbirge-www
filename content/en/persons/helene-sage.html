Hélène Sage is a singer, musician, actress, director, and composer. She assists and advises numerous artists and voice professionals. She is a member of the French Association of Voice Teachers (AFPC) and has developed a particular passion for the research of Jo Estill (certified at Levels 1 and 2 in the “Estill Voice Training System” since 2008) and for Daïnouri Choque’s work on listening to harmonics.
As early as 1981, she took part in the great orchestra of Un Drama Musical Instantané and became the fourth episodic member of the band.
In 2018, the Austrian label Klang Galerie published the duo Appointment she recorded in 1981 with Jean-Jacques Birgé (who participated in several of her albums).
