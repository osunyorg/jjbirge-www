After training in psychology experimental, a decade of painting and a few years to go artist Nicolas Clauss poses brushes in 2000 to continue his painting work through video and code IT. His installations and video paintings are works non-figured, in constant "re-write". In a form of visual anthropology (sometimes choreographic), his approach probes the figure and human reality by inventing other modes of exploration of the moving image, notably through the randomisation algorithmic.
