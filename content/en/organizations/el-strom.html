El Strøm means the current in Danish.
Three streams form a river.
At the first dam electric energy favors travel. Birgitte Lyregaard sings while mocking borders (jazz, pop, punk, contemporary, ... to fly over the continents in mad fire. Babylon in a Handkerchief.
Sacha Gattano kitchen sharp rhythms and sound objects picked on the planet, transforming his toys with the gravity of the children who take to the game.
Jean-Jacques Birgé, who plays here of rare instruments, acoustic or electronic, says it is the First time he's met so much complicity since A Drama Instant Musical.
Hand-sewn songs or free improvisation, Homemade instruments or state-of-the-art technology, mermaid singing or machine revolt, electroshock produces a good mood more True as nature.
