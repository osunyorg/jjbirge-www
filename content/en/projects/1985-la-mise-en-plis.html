body: >-


Michèle BUIRETTE (accordion) invites Bruno GIRARD (violin), Didier PETIT (cello), Geneviève CABANNES (bass), Dominique FONFRÈDE (voice), Hélène SAGE (bass, voice), Jean-Jacques BIRGÉ (synthesizer, percussion, trombone), Francis GORGÉ (synthesizer, percussion, guitar) for a beautiful record just before trio Pied de Poule.

 

Manel, Enzyme, Le chevalier à la triste figure by the group L'arme à l'œil

Recording and mix – Jean-Jacques Birgé Painting by Patrice Roger after a photo by Michel Gombart Photos of the musicians – Thierry Derhesdin


n°1 / Pas de fumée sans feu / 4'00
Michèle Buirette – accordion Music MB

n°2 / Agoraphobie / 9'45
Michèle Buirette – accordion Francis Gorgé – synthesizer, percussion, guitar ; waltz Jean-Jacques Birgé – synthesizer, percussion, trombone, jaw harp ; scenery Musicians of the waltz : Hélène Sage – flute Bernard Vitet – trumpet Didier Petit – cello Geneviève Cabannes – keyboard, double bass Music MB-FG-JJB

n°3 / Manel / 5'50
Michèle Buirette – accordion Bruno Girard – violin Didier Petit – cello Music BG

n°4 / Enzyme / 3'05
Didier Petit – cello Michèle Buirette – accordion Bruno Girard – violin Trad. Arr. MB-BG-DP

n°5 / Le devis de l'amour / 7'05
Dominique Fonfrède – vocals Michèle Buirette – accordion Hélène Sage – double bass, vocals (DF after Clément Marot / HS-DF-MB)

n°6 / La moëlle chemin de fer / 3'15
Michèle Buirette – accordions Music MB

n°7 / Le chevalier à la triste figure / 6'20
Bruno Girard – violin, vocals Didier Petit – cello Michèle Buirette – accordion Words & music MB
