body: >-


Michèle Buirette sings and plays accordion, her own texts or those by Gigi Bigot ... She composes surrealistic songs, often funny and tender, about our daily universe which is also absurd and brutal, sometimes about childhood and what is left of it into each of us. She actually plays live, solo or trio with Max Robin and Moïra Montier-Dauriac, and with the show Comment ça va sur la terre ? with Elsa Birgé and Linda Edsjö...
