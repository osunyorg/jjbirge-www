body: >-


Instant compositions by all the protagonists recorded and mixed at Studio GRRR, Bagnolet, by Jean-Jacques Birgé, first among ourselves in 2023 (indexes 1 to 4), then from 2024 in public (indexes 5 to 9) under the title APÉRO LABO.

It's all about playing to meet each other, not the other way around as is customary. The virtual albums from which these creations originate are available exclusively online on this site or on Bandcamp . The theme of each piece is drawn just before the game.

Graphic design – Étienne Mineur who made the beautiful video-clip 72 minutes Official issue on April 18th 2025 French article on Blog Already on Bandcamp


n°1 / Vers l'insignifiant / 4'47
Emmanuelle Legros – trumpet, percussion Matthieu Donarier – tenor sax Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, jaw harp, reed trumpet, saw duang

n°2 / L'offrande / 8'44
Hélène Duret – clarinet, bass clarinet, vocals & body Rafaelle Rinaudo – electric harp Jean-Jacques Birgé – Terra, Enner, keyboard

n°3 / Don't Be Frightened of Cliches / 12'30
Isabel Sörling – electric guitar Maëlle Desbrosses – double bass Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard

n°4 / Célèbre ton erreur comme une intention cachée / 12'50
Bruno Ducret – cello, guitar, cosmic bow, vocals Olivia Scemama – electric bass ukulele Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, percussion, harmonicas, jaw harp, reeds

n°5 / Yemen / 5'53
Mathias Lévy – violin Antonin-Tri Hoang – synthesizer, percussion Jean-Jacques Birgé – Enner, sampler

n°6 / Codex / 7'01
Maëlle Desbrosses – percussion, birdcalls, viola Fanny Meteier – tuba, vocals Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, megaphone, Enner

n°7 / Is it finished? / 10'07
Fabiana Striffler – violin, harmonica Léa Ciechelski – alto sax Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard

n°8 / Pour qui sonne le glas / 2'50
Hélène Duret – bass clarinet Alexandre Saada – piano Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard

n°9 / D'une fourchette / 7'32
Catherine Delaunay – clarinet Roberto Negro – piano, whirly tube Jean-Jacques Birgé – synthesizer, keyboard, jaw harp
