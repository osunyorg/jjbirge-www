GRRR 3054 online, 2006/2007

body: >-


In 2006 and 2007 Jean-Jacques Birgé and Nicolas Clauss project their Somnambules on the screen of Le Triton at Les Lilas, inviting other musicians to join them. Until then the composer and the painter used to play as a duet when they were playing live ( article on French blog ). On January 28 2006 singer Pascale Labbé and Didier Petit on cello are in. On May 3 2007 it is Étienne Brunet on various wind instruments and drummer Éric Échampard ( another article on Birgé's blog ). All are Birgé's long time firends and have played with him on various occasions. 1h33
