GRRR 3112 online, 2023

body: >-


Instantaneous compositions recorded on May 30 2023 at GRRR Studio by Jean-Jacques Birgé in the order which they have been played chosing cards from Oblique Strategies conceived by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt

 

Considering the particular instrumentation of this album - a wind bass and a string bass - we strongly advise you to listen to it on good speakers or headphones!

91 minutes

Also on Bandcamp

Cover & photos © JJB

 

As for albums Game Bling with Ève Risser and Joce Mienniel (2014), Un coup de dés jamais n'abolira le hasard with Médéric Collignon and Julien Desprez (2014),  Un coup de dés jamais n'abolira le hasard 2 with Pascal Contet and Antonin-Tri Hoang (2015), Questions with Élise Dabrowski and Mathias Lévy (May 2019),  WD-40 with Christelle Séry and Jonathan Pontier (Oct. 2019), Ball of Fire with Jean-François Vrod and Karsten Hochapfel (Nov. 2019), L’air de rien with Élise Caron and Fidel Fourneyron (mars 2021), To Be or Net To Be with Gilles Coronado and Basile Naudet (mai 2021), Chou with Sophie Agnel et David Fenech (October 2022), and the live show filmed À l'improviste with Birgitte Lyregaard and Linda Edsjö (2014), cards from the game Oblique Strategies by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt were randomly chosen as scores for the musicians.

 

Films of some concerts, where the audience choses the cards, are also online on YouTube (with Collignon, Desprez, Contet, Hoang, Lyregaard, Edsjö)...


n°1 / Go to an extreme, move back to a more comfortable place / 7'50
Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards Fanny Meteier – tuba Olivier Lété – electric bass

Jean-Jacques Birgé – reed trumpet, keyboards, percussion, jaw’s harp, enner Fanny Meteier – tuba Olivier Lété – electric bass

Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards Fanny Meteier – tuba Olivier Lété – electric bass

n°4 / Don’t break the silence / 5'18
Jean-Jacques Birgé –keyboards Fanny Meteier – pages, fingers, tuba Olivier Lété – electric bass

Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, bull’s roar Fanny Meteier – tuba Olivier Lété – electric bass

n°6 / Sans carte / 13'06
Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, H3000 Fanny Meteier – tuba Olivier Lété – electric bass

n°7 / Turn it upside down / 12'40
Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, jaw’s harp, reed Fanny Meteier – tuba, gédéon   Olivier Lété – electric bass

Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, whistle, jaw’s harp, drum, erhu, music box Fanny Meteier – tin guitar, tuba, percussion, nose Olivier Lété – electric bass

Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, flute, energy chimes, zither Fanny Meteier – tuba Olivier Lété – electric bass
