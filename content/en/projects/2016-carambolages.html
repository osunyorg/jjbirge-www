Score of the exhibition CARAMBOLAGES at Grand Palais, Paris organised by the Réunion des Musées Nationaux March 2 - July 4 2016 Curator : Jean-Hubert Martin Sound and musical score : Jean-Jacques Birgé (orchestra, keyboards, vocals, electronics, percussion, field recording) with participation of Vincent Segal (cello) and Antonin-Tri Hoang (alto sax, clarinet, bass clarinet, piano) who composed 11 of the 27 pieces together with Birgé. Played as loops on a free application for smartphone (iOS and Android) with your own headphones synchronized with the whole exhibition and with the catalogue... Articles on French Blog : 1.
