GRRR 3113 online, 2023

body: >-


Instantaneous compositions recorded on June 8 2023 at GRRR Studio by Jean-Jacques Birgé in the order which they have been played chosing cards from Oblique Strategies conceived by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt

  68 minutes

Also on Bandcamp

Cover & photos © JJB

 

As for albums Game Bling with Ève Risser and Joce Mienniel (2014), Un coup de dés jamais n'abolira le hasard with Médéric Collignon and Julien Desprez (2014),  Un coup de dés jamais n'abolira le hasard 2 with Pascal Contet and Antonin-Tri Hoang (2015), Questions with Élise Dabrowski and Mathias Lévy (May 2019),  WD-40 with Christelle Séry and Jonathan Pontier (Oct. 2019), Ball of Fire with Jean-François Vrod and Karsten Hochapfel (Nov. 2019), L’air de rien with Élise Caron and Fidel Fourneyron (mars 2021), To Be or Net To Be with Gilles Coronado and Basile Naudet (mai 2021), Chou with Sophie Agnel and David Fenech (October 2022), Raves with Olivier Lété and Fanny Meteier (May 2023), and the live show filmed À l'improviste with Birgitte Lyregaard and Linda Edsjö (2014), cards from the game Oblique Strategies by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt were randomly chosen as scores for the musicians.

 

Films of some concerts, where the audience choses the cards, are also online on YouTube (with Collignon, Desprez, Contet, Hoang, Lyregaard, Edsjö)...


n°1 / Give The Game Away / 20'48
Violaine Lochu – vocals Tatiana Paris – electric guitar Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, Enner, jaw harps, flute

n°2 / Give Way To Your Worst Impulse / 18'21
Violaine Lochu – vocals Tatiana Paris – electric guitar Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, brake, flute, bullroarer, whistle Words from Les Guérillères by Monique Wittig

n°3 / What To Maintain? / 15'14
Violaine Lochu – vocals Tatiana Paris – electric guitar Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, Lyra-8, flute, reed trumpet, jaw harp Words from Les Guérillères by Monique Wittig

n°4 / Allow An Easement (an easement is the abandonment of a scripture) / 7'06
Violaine Lochu – vocals Tatiana Paris – electric guitar, damaru Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, Tenori-on Words from L'argent by Christophe Tarkos

n°5 / Voice Nagging Suspicions / 6'49
Violaine Lochu – vocals Tatiana Paris – megaphone, smartphone, amplifier Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, The Pipe Words from La quête infinie de l'autre rive by Sylvie Kandé
