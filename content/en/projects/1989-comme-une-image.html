"Surrealistic tracks full of humor and eclectism." (Fnac Guide) Music for ballet on one side (pour Nadj, Tompkins, Gaudin). Baudelaire, Duparc, Goethe, Schubert, Mallarmé on same side ! With Hélène SAGE, Bernard VITET, Mark TOMPKINS, Gérard GOURDOT, Michèle BUIRETTE, Daniel LALOUX, Jean-Jacques BIRGÉ, Francis GORGÉ, Elodie GONZALEZ, Ismel DA SILVEIRA, Karine SAPORTA Artistic director - Bernard Vitet Photo Philippe - Noiré


n°1 / L'invitation au voyage / 7'47
Hélène Sage – vocals, piano (Baudelaire-Duparc) 1989

n°2 / Les sept merveilles du monde / 3'35
Jean-Jacques Birgé – trombone, jaw harp, inanga, vocals, reed trumpet, harmonica, mukawina Hélène Sage – bicycle, stool, flute, drums, kettle, double bass, prepared piano (Birgé-Sage) 1989

Michèle Buirette – accordéon Hélène Sage – organ Extract from Josef Nadj's ballet Sept peaux de rhinocéros (Buirette-Sage) 1988

n°4 / Bal sombre / 6'06
Daniel Laloux – drum Hélène Sage – organ Extract from Josef Nadj's ballet Sept peaux de rhinocéros (Laloux-Sage) 1988

n°5 / Un coup de dés / 6'36
In order of appearance : Bernard Vitet, Hélène Sage, La mouette, Elodie Gonzalez, Philippe Noire, Gérard Gourdot, Jean-Yves Bouchicot, Francine Ferrer, Jean Toscane, Lulla Card, Maryline Carton, Anne Fournier, Hélène Lassalle, Martha Moore, Isnel Da Silveira, Maryse Gautier, Caroline Vitet, Anne Bouchicot, Claude Perrin, Didier Silhol, Marianne Bono, Mistinguette, Léon Noiret, Jean-Jacques Birgé, Jean-Bernard Ducos, Lucienne Vitet, Michèle Buirette, Mark Tompkins, Jean Gaudin, Jean Danielou and the laughing birds (Stéphane Mallarmé/Vitet-Sage) Extracts 1985 à 1989

n°6 / Erl König / 5'05
Bernard Vitet – vocals Hélène Sage – piano (Johan Wolfgang von Goethe/Franz Schubert) 1989

n°7 / Comment faire apparaître un enfer / 5'13
Léon Noiret – vocals Hélène Sage – organ (Nicola Sabattini/Hélène Sage) 1988

n°8 / Le rêve du comédon / 0'55
Gérard Gourdot – vocals Hélène Sage – piccolo, percussion (Gourdot-Sage) 1989

n°9 / La nuit n'a dormi que d'un œil / 4'12
Hélène Lassalle – laughing Elodie Gonzalez, Isnel Da Silveira, Mark Tompkins – vocals Hélène Sage – original instruments, vocals, percussion (Sage) 1986

n°10 / Fric et fracas / 4'05
Élodie Gonzalez – vocals Francis Gorgé – synthesizer, digital percussion Hélène Sage – vocals, winds, acoustic percussion (Gorgé-Sage) 1986

n°11 / Un matin de coccinelle / 0'13
Hélène Sage – vocals 1985

n°12 / Il n'y eut pas de remède / 3'55
Bernard Vitet, Karine Saporta, François Liège, Francis Gorgé – vocals, footsteps Hélène Sage – organ, vocals, footsteps (Sage) 1983-1987

n°13 / Tempête à la Tour Eiffel / 5'39
Daniel Laloux – drum Mark Tompkins – vocals Hélène Sage – double bass, percussion, vocals Composed Mark Tompkins' ballet Haute trahison and also played during Récital of the duo Tompkins and Sage (Tompkins/Tompkins-Sage) 1986

n°14 / Fanfare tupamarose / 1'03
Bernard Vitet – flugelhorn, trumpet, sliding bells, drums Hélène Sage – tenor sax, piccolo, effects, percussion (Vitet-Sage) 1989
