Improvisations recorded on March 6 2014 at Studio GRRR
chosing cards from Oblique Strategies conceived by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt
Edited and mixed by Jean-Jacques Birgé

First episode of "Un coup de dés jamais n'abolira le hasard"
(next ones have been played live at Radio France, at l'Atelier du Plateau
on Nov. 10 and 15 2014 with Danish singer Birgitte Lyregaard
and Swedish percussionist Linda Edsjö,
then it will be played on Nov. 28 at Le Triton with Médéric Collignon
on trumpet and Julien Desprez on guitar)
