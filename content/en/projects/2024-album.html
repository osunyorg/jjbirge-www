GRRR 3122 online, 2024

body: >-


Instantaneous compositions recorded live on October 13, 2024 at Studio GRRR by Jean-Jacques Birgé in the order they were played, young Eliott randomly pulling pages from the Album book, photographs selected by Gabriel Bauret and Grégoire Solotareff, published by L'école des loisirs, as themes for the improvisations

“I gave this book to his mother when she herself was a child!” (JJB)

Cover photo by Manuel Cuadrada, 1950

 

65 minutes
