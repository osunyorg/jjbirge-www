Fréquence de nuit "Dark Night"
on the air on July 1st 1983 - France Musique
Evening coordinated by Didier Alluard and Monique Veaute
Radio show produced by Un drame musical instantané :
Jean-Jacques Birgé, Bernard Vitet and Francis Gorgé
Sound - Alain Nedelec
Assistants - Bernard Treton, Christine Bessely
Effects - Dominique Auber
Original music by Un Drame Musical Instantané
with
