GRRR 1006 lp, 1983

body: >-


"Drawing on everything from chamber music to melodrama scoring to experimental improvisation and noise, the music creates a panorama quite conductive for the listeners to create their own episodes on..." (Cadence) A wonderful cover by Jean Bruller (a.k.a. Vercors). L'invitation au voyage by Charles Baudelaire and Henri Duparc, and Youenn Le Berre's bagpipes. A suite of nightmares and Copernician revolutions.

Music and words by Un Drame Musical Instantané (Birgé–Vitet–Gorgé) except L’invitation au voyage (Baudelaire–Duparc-arr.Un d.m.i.) and Sacra Matao (Le Berre)

Live recording by Daniel Deshays at Studio Berthelot, Montreuil

November 30th and December 1st 1982

Drawings by Jean Bruller

Photo by Philippe Monteillet


n°1 / Ne pas être admiré, être cru / 16'16
Francis Gorgé - electric guitar Jean-Jacques Birgé - waldteufel, trumpet, flute, keyboard, jaw harp (1), inanga (2), reed trumpet, tape Bernard Vitet - flugel horn, voice, horn, reed trumpet, conductor Hélène Sage - bass flute, flute, vocals Jacques Marugg - marimba, vibraphone, tympani Gérard Siracusa - percussion, bells Patrice Petitdidier - French horn, horn Philippe Legris - tuba, horn Jean Querlier - oboe, flute, alto sax Youenn Le Berre - bassoon, flute, G flute, tenor sax Bruno Girard - violin Nathalie Baudoin - viola Didier Petit - cello Hélène Bass - cello Geneviève Cabannes - double bass Jacques Bidou - recorded voice

n°2 / L'invitation au voyage + Sacra Matao / 8'20
from Concerto de la Lune Bernard Vitet - vocals Jean Querlier - oboe, English horn Francis Gorgé - guitar Jean-Jacques Birgé - synthesizer Bruno Girard - violin, recorded voice Nathalie Baudoin - viola Hélène Bass - cello Didier Petit - cello Geneviève Cabannes - double bass Patrice Petitdidier - post horn, French horn Philippe Legris - tuba Hélène Sage - jazzoflutes Gérard Siracusa - conductor Youenn Le Berre - bagpipes Marianne Bonneau - recorded voice

for 3 orchestras Un Jour : Jean-Jacques Birgé - conductor, piano, voice, tapes, percussion Hélène Sage - flute, boiling pot, percussion, voice Bruno Girard - violin, voice Hélène Bass - cello, voice Le Canon : Francis Gorgé - conductor Jean Querlier - English horn Youenn Le Berre - bassoon Jacques Marugg - vibraphone, marimba, tympani Nathalie Baudoin - viola Didier Petit - cello Geneviève Cabannes - double bass Tonnerre : Bernard Vitet - conductor, flugel horn, horn Patrice Petitdidier - French horn, horn Philippe Legris - tuba, horn Gérard Siracusa - percussion, bells, horn
