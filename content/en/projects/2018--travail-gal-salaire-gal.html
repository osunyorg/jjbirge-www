Words and music by Un Drame Musical Instantané (Birgé–Vitet–Gorgé)

Recorded in 1981-82 À Travail Égal Salaire Égal is Un Drame Musical Instantané's third album, originally released in 1982 on the group's own GRRR label. You will hear their wild mix of styles, a very original, unclassifiable, inventive music, which drifts from jazz to electronic music and contemporary music. Remastered in 2017 with unissued bonus track for Austrian label Klang Galerie. Article on French Blog
