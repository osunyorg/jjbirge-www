Score of the exhibition CARAMBOLAGES at Grand Palais, Paris organised by the Réunion des Musées Nationaux March 2 - July 4 2016 Curator : Jean-Hubert Martin Sound and musical score : Jean-Jacques Birgé (orchestra, keyboards, vocals, electronics, percussion, field recording) with participation of Vincent Segal (cello) and Antonin-Tri Hoang (alto sax, clarinet, bass clarinet, piano) who composed 11 of the 27 pieces together with Birgé. Played as loops on a free application for smartphone (iOS and Android) with your own headphones synchronized with the whole exhibition and with the catalogue... You can follow the sound of the 27 rooms with the numbers given below. Articles on French Blog : 1. Le regard / 2. Synchronisme et mp3 / 3. Suivez le guide / 4. Le parcours sonore / 5.
