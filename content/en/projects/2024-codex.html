body: >-


Instantaneous compositions recorded live on February 18, 2024 at Studio GRRR by Jean-Jacques Birgé in the order they were played and belonging to the APÉRO LABO 2 series, inspired by pages from Luigi Serafini's Codex Seraphinianus chosen by the audience

Maëlle Desbrosses et Fanny Meteier belong to the duet MÉTÉORE

 

57 minutes

 

Also on Bandcamp

Article by Vanessa Paparella on Citizen Jazz

Jean-Jacques Birgé introduces the Codex Seraphinianus

Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, waldteufel, cabuletê, inanga Maëlle Desbrosses – viola Fanny Meteier – tuba

Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, reed trumpet Maëlle Desbrosses – door harp, crossbow Fanny Meteier – tuba

Jean-Jacques Birgé – Terra, music box, keyboard Maëlle Desbrosses – vocals, viola Fanny Meteier – tuba

Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, bell, effect Maëlle Desbrosses – viola Fanny Meteier – Gedeon, tuba
