LP "Surprise-Partie avec Bernard Vitet"
(Guilde Européenne du Disque SP53)
Article on French Blog (external link)
1954 ?

Bernard Vitet - trombone
Bib Monville - tenor sax
Bob Aubert - guitar
Sadi Lallemand - vibraphone, marimba, bongos
Pierre Franzini - piano
Pierre Sim (?) - double bass
Baptiste "Mac Kak" Reilles (?) - drums

Side 1
Oye Mambo (mambo by Trianda), Dansero (bolero by Haymann), Crazy Rythm (mambo-guaracha by Meyer), Pielcanella (by Capo, written on LP, but was it recorded?!), Temptation (bolero by Brown), Starling Rye (calypso by S.Sid), Toi qui disais (fox by Suesse).
Side 2
Le loup, la biche et le chevalier (calypso by Henri Salvador), I got you under my skin (bolero by Cole Porter), Dimanche (fox by Bib Monville), Jokin' the blues (fox by Vitet) and Isabel Day (slow by Bob Aubert), but there is again one title too many!
Plus...
Compilation C'est arrivé au Whisky à Gogo (GEM151) features 2 more pieces: Du soleil plein la tête (fox de Crolla) and Lundi (fox de Monville)
