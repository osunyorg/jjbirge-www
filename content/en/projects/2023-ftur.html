GRRR 3117 online, 2023

body: >-


36th album of the series "Pique-nique au labo" It’s about playing to meet and not the other way around as usual. The theme of each piece was randomly chosen just before playing.

 

Instantaneous compositions recorded on November 1 2023 at GRRR Studio by Jean-Jacques Birgé in the order which they have been played chosing cards from Oblique Strategies conceived by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt

 

Etymologically, the word potiron (pumpkin) could come from the Arabic fǔtur "mushroom", via Jewish or Arab physicians!

80 minutes

Also on Bandcamp

 

French Blog

Photos © JJB

 

Cards from the game Oblique Strategies by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt were randomly chosen as scores for the musicians, as for albums Game Bling with Ève Risser and Joce Mienniel (2014), Un coup de dés jamais n'abolira le hasard with Médéric Collignon and Julien Desprez (2014),  Un coup de dés jamais n'abolira le hasard 2 with Pascal Contet and Antonin-Tri Hoang (2015), Questions with Élise Dabrowski and Mathias Lévy (May 2019),  WD-40 with Christelle Séry and Jonathan Pontier (Oct. 2019), Ball of Fire with Jean-François Vrod and Karsten Hochapfel (Nov. 2019), L’air de rien with Élise Caron and Fidel Fourneyron (mars 2021), To Be or Net To Be with Gilles Coronado and Basile Naudet (mai 2021), Chou with Sophie Agnel and David Fenech (October 2022), Raves with Olivier Lété and Fanny Meteier (May 2023), Moite with Violaine Lochu and Tatiana Paris (July 2023), Par terre with Emmanuelle Legros and Matthieu Donarier (July 2023), Listen To The Quiet Plattfisk with Isabel Sörling et Maëlle Desbrosses (October 2023), and the live show filmed À l'improviste with Birgitte Lyregaard and Linda Edsjö (2014).

 

Films of some concerts, where the audience choses the cards, are also online on YouTube (with Collignon, Desprez, Contet, Hoang, Lyregaard, Edsjö)...


n°1 / Le principe d'incohérence / 4'04
Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, Enner Bruno Ducret – cello Olivia Scemama – electric bass ukulele

n°2 / Humanisez un sans faute / 5'57
Jean-Jacques Birgé – Tenori-on, keyboards Bruno Ducret – cello Olivia Scemama – electric bass ukulele

n°3 / Essayez de faire semblant / 18'50
Jean-Jacques Birgé – reed trumpets, keyboards, Cosmos, Terra, effects Bruno Ducret – Calabrian lira, vocals, cello Olivia Scemama – electric bass ukulele

n°4 / Célèbre ton erreur comme une intention cachée / 16'27
Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, percussion, harmonicas, jaw harp, reeds, Terra, vocals, erhu Bruno Ducret – cello, guitar, cosmic bow, vocals Olivia Scemama – electric bass ukulele

n°5 / Vers l'insignifiant / 10'00
Jean-Jacques Birgé – flute, jaw harp, keyboards, percussion Bruno Ducret – cello Olivia Scemama – small gong, electric bass ukulele

n°6 / Distorsion du temps / 24'30
Jean-Jacques Birgé – Terra, frogs, lotto, keyboards, The Pipe, effects Bruno Ducret – cello, La Bandera, vocals, trumpet Olivia Scemama – electric bass ukulele
