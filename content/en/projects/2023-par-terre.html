GRRR 3114 online, 2023

body: >-


Instantaneous compositions recorded on July 11 2023 at GRRR Studio by Jean-Jacques Birgé in the order which they have been played chosing cards from Oblique Strategies conceived by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt

  60 minutes

Also on Bandcamp

Photos : the cover © Matthieu Donarier the studio © Emmanuelle Legros the trio © JJB

 

As for albums Game Bling with Ève Risser and Joce Mienniel (2014), Un coup de dés jamais n'abolira le hasard with Médéric Collignon and Julien Desprez (2014),  Un coup de dés jamais n'abolira le hasard 2 with Pascal Contet and Antonin-Tri Hoang (2015), Questions with Élise Dabrowski and Mathias Lévy (May 2019),  WD-40 with Christelle Séry and Jonathan Pontier (Oct. 2019), Ball of Fire with Jean-François Vrod and Karsten Hochapfel (Nov. 2019), L’air de rien with Élise Caron and Fidel Fourneyron (mars 2021), To Be or Net To Be with Gilles Coronado and Basile Naudet (mai 2021), Chou with Sophie Agnel and David Fenech (October 2022), Raves with Olivier Lété and Fanny Meteier (May 2023), Moite with Violaine Lochu and Tatiana Paris (July 2023), and the live show filmed À l'improviste with Birgitte Lyregaard and Linda Edsjö (2014), cards from the game Oblique Strategies by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt were randomly chosen as scores for the musicians.

 

Films of some concerts, where the audience choses the cards, are also online on YouTube (with Collignon, Desprez, Contet, Hoang, Lyregaard, Edsjö)...


n°1 / Vers l'insignifiant / 4'47
Emmanuelle Legros – trumpet, percussion Matthieu Donarier – tenor sax Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, jaw harp, reed trumpet, saw duang

n°2 / La chose la plus importante est la chose qu'on oublie le plus facilement / 7'21
Emmanuelle Legros – flugel horn, percussion Matthieu Donarier – tenor sax Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, field recording, flute

n°3 / Slow preparation, fast execution / 8'24
Emmanuelle Legros – trumpet, percussion Matthieu Donarier – tenor sax, percussion Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboards, vocals, flute, reed trumpet

n°4 / Toujours les premiers pas / 8'07
Emmanuelle Legros – African horn, flugel horn Matthieu Donarier – tenor sax Jean-Jacques Birgé – percussion, flute, jaw harp, keyboard

n°5 / Trois couleurs inacceptables / 10'14
Emmanuelle Legros – trumpet, flugel horn, percussion Matthieu Donarier – tenor sax Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, reed, Lyra-8, Enner

n°6 / Continuez comme ça / 4'08
Emmanuelle Legros – music box, percussion Matthieu Donarier – tenor sax, toy-piano Jean-Jacques Birgé – electronic drums, vocals, inanga

n°7 / Une partie seulement, pas tout / 5'13
Emmanuelle Legros – trumpet Matthieu Donarier – tenor sax, percussion Jean-Jacques Birgé – violin, reed trumpet, flute

n°8 / Dans l'obscurité ou une très grande salle, silencieusement / 12'35
Emmanuelle Legros – flugel horn Matthieu Donarier – tenor sax Jean-Jacques Birgé – keyboard, Tenori-on, percussion, nose flute, saw u
