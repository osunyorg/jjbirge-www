The beginning. Totally improvised. Magnifically structured. Features a percussion trio on a text by Jean Vigo. A reference. Jean-Jacques BIRGÉ (synthesizer, vocals, flutes, percussion) Bernard VITET (trumpets, reeds, violin, percussion) Francis GORGÉ (guitar, bass guitar, percussion) Prenons-le / Au pied de la lettre / Trop d'adrénaline nuit + Sancta Papaverina (bonus track on cd) lp GRRR 1002 - lp version out of sale rec. 1977 - prod. 1979

IMPORTANT: REISSUE NOW AVAILABLE (GRRR 2024) new master with bonus track ( Sancta Papaverina - 17'44") and a new graphic design by Etienne AUGER A GIFT! The pieces you are listening to are two unissued version before editing, far longer than the ones on the LP which lasts 9 and 24 minutes. From the same period, 24 hours of unissued music on Poisons ... Also on BANDCAMP


n°1 / Prenons le (05/02/1977) / 9'20
Jean-Jacques Birgé - percussion, clarina, inanga Bernard Vitet - flugel horn, French horn Francis Gorgé - guitar, bass guitar, violin

n°2 / La gangrène a.k.a. Au pied de la lettre (1977/03/08) / 23'30
Jean-Jacques Birgé - percussions + vocals ( Tout homme détient dans ses mains son destin ), saw, synthesizer, alto saxophone, jaw harp, mandoline Bernard Vitet - percussions + vocals ( Un coup de dés jamais n'abolit le hasard ), violin, zheng Francis Gorgé - percussions + electric guitar and basse Hommage to Jean Vigo.

n°3 / Trop d'adrénaline nuit (1977/01/16, complete) / 41'51
Jean-Jacques Birgé - melodica alto & soprano, synthesizer, vocals, tape ( Le chemin de Rio ), alto saxophone, violin, plastic trumpet, hou-kin, bird toy, percussion, tenor flute, Bb trumpet, tricolore, jazzflute, jaw harp, senza ikembé Bernard Vitet - flugel horn, Bb trumpet, plastic clarinet & saxophone, percussion, violin, alto saxophone, bird toy, vocals, toy-piano, mandoline, elecric bass Francis Gorgé - elecric bass & guitar,horn , plastic trumpet LP and CD versions are only 24'25 long.

n°4 / Sancta Papaverina (01/16/1977) / 17'44
Jean-Jacques Birgé - synthesizer (ARP 2600), record player, harmonica, jazzoflute, jaw harp, percussion Bernard Vitet - piccolo trumpet, flugel horn, French horn, whistle, reeds, percussion Francis Gorgé - bass guitar, guitar Unissued on the original LP.
