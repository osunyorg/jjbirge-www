body: >-


Two novels by Dino Buzzati , music by Un Drame Musical Instantané ! Le K and Jeune fille qui tombe, tombe... have been first created in Montreuil on December 20 1985 with Michael Lonsdale . Gérard Siracusa was replacing Francis Gorgé. Two new versions were created in 1990 with two new actors. A CD of Le K has been published by GRRR in 1990 with Richard Bohringer and the trio Birgé-Vitet-Gorgé. Reissued in 1993 by Auvidis (incl. a shorter instrumental) Le K has been nominated at 9e Victoires de la Musique. A CD of Jeune fille qui tombe, tombe... has been issued by in situ in 1991 with Daniel Laloux who performed also Le K on stage, after Richard Bohringer. Here are three unissued versions, but all still in French. 2h40 Poster - Raymond Sarti
