Jean-Jacques Birgé plays on two CD by Hélène Sage, Comme une image and Les araignées . Polyinstrumentist Hélène Sage has belonged to the 15 piece-orchestra of Un Drame Musical Instantané from its creation until its dissolution. She has played with Birgé, Vitet, Gorgé, as duets, sometimes as a quartet as in Trou one can listen here in the unissued concerts by Un D.M.I. Dedicated to Bri and Pere ;-) 2h34


n°1 / La Tramontane 1 - 1982 / 33'15
Jean-Jacques Birgé - flute, pocket trumpet, trombone, reed trumpet, synthesizer Hélène Sage - rainstick, flute in C, piccolo flute, bass clarinet who present their instruments after each improvisation with Catalan translation... Recorded live outside by night at Ordis, Catalonia, 1982

Jean-Jacques Birgé - synthesizer, senza ikembé, jaw harp, ambiances, Ordis church bells, reed trumpet, alto saxophone, flutes Hélène Sage - music box, piano as a percussion and string instrument who present their instruments after each improvisation with Catalan translation... Recorded live outside by night at Ordis, Catalonia, 1982

n°7 / Paris Music Fest in a 2CV - 1982 / 34'56
Jean-Jacques Birgé - flutes, reeds, percussion, electronics Hélène Sage - flutes, reeds, percussion Brigitte Dornès - driving Marianne Bonneau - recording On June 1982, for the 1st Music Fest in Paris, we had equiped Brigitte's 2CV Citrën with a megaphone, we had opened its roof and we were standing playing through it with musicians in the streets or at their window.
