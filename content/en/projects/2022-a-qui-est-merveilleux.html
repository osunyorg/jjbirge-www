Dominique Fonfrède - vocals (improvisations, texts) Françoise Toullec - piano (improvisations, compositions) Recorded and mixed on September 2021

Image - Trax

 

"Ça qui est merveilleux" (Oh this is a happy day), an expression borrowed from Beckett, a deliciously poetic humor, the art of finding in the slightest thing, be it light or poignant, a source of great pleasure.

 

After Dramaticules (Beckett inspires them definitely), here is the 2nd album of the duo, chiseled from improvisations long reworked, structured and close to the composition. In the musical field of the extended piano, the texts come sometimes to impose their own dynamics; they are then conceived like the characters of a true comedy.
