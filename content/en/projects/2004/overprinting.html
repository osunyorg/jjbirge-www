body: >-


This album has never been finished nor released due to misunderstanding
betwwen Israelian musicians and Un Drame Musical Instantané. Meidad
Zaharia then stopped his production activities and the pieces remained
unpublished. The album should have been structured in two parts, each
ensemble of musicians recording a playback for the other. Un Drame
Musical Instantanté did send 4 pieces for Ganelin's orchestra to record
over it, and the other side should have been called Pépé le Moko ? . Recorded at Studio GRRR and in Tel Aviv. 61 minutes

This album has never been finished nor released due to misunderstanding betwwen Israelian musicians and Un Drame Musical Instantané. Meidad Zaharia then stopped his production activities and the pieces remained unpublished. The album should have been structured in two parts, each ensemble of musicians recording a playback for the other. Un Drame Musical Instantanté did send 4 pieces for Ganelin's orchestra to record over it, and the other side should have been called Pépé le Moko ?.
Recorded at Studio GRRR and in Tel Aviv.
61 minutes
