A blue cover by painter Jacques Monory. The last LP by Un D.M.I. The trio with guests and a large orchestra. Plus electronics, Amazonian fiction, crazy rock, vocal noises, dirty war, silent phone and a symphonic poem ! Best reissue of 2021 (Franpi Sunship) Jean-Jacques BIRGÉ (synthesizer, vocals, piano, brass, reeds, percussion) Bernard VITET (trumpets, vocals, violin, reeds) Francis GORGÉ (guitars, synthesizer, vocals, percussion, break) with Jean QUERLIER (oboe, English horn, flute, soprano sax) Youenn LE BERRE (flutes, bassoon) Patrice PETITDIDIER (French horn) Michèle BUIRETTE (accordion) Geneviève CABANNES (double-bass) Youval MICENMACHER (percussion) and Instrumental Ensemble of the NOUVEL ORCHESTRE PHILHARMONIQUE conducted by Yves PRIN La fièvre verte / Les gueules cassées / La Bourse et la vie / Rangé des voitures / Cabine 13 / Le téléphone muet / Passage à l'acte + Extra track :  La Bourse et la vie (original pure unissued mix with introduction by Marc Monnet, 20 minutes)
