Nothing comparable, maybe except the Liberation Orchestra ! This album, inspired by Abdulah SIDRAN 's poems, puts together fifty artists under the artistic direction of Jean-Jacques BIRGÉ and Corinne LÉONET :

Lindsay COOPER, Henri TEXIER, Dee Dee BRIDGEWATER, le Quatuor BALANESCU, Willem BREUKER, Louis SCLAVIS, Pierre CHARIAL, Mike et Kate WESTBROOK, Linda SHARROCK, Wolfgang PUSCHNIG, Jane BIRKIN, Bulle OGIER, André DUSSOLLIER, Un Drame Musical Instantané, Phil MINTON, Bruno CHEVILLON, Chris BISCOE, Noël AKCHOTÉ, Sébastien TEXIER, Bojan Z, Tony RABESON, Thomas BLOCH, Gérard SIRACUSA, Michèle BUIRETTE, Jean-Jacques BIRGÉ, Bernard VITET, Dean BRODRICK, Brian ABRAHAMS, Carol ROBINSON, Michel GODARD, Emil KRISTOF, Lorre Lynn TRYTTEN, Richard HAYON, Abdulah SIDRAN…

At the benefit of the reconstruction of the National Library of Sarajevo. English, French and Bosnian words.

 

Texts from Abdulah Sidran's Sarajevski Tabut © Bosanska Knjiga, Sarajevo 1993 French translation by Mireille Robin Cercueil de Sarajevo © Ed. Francis Bueb et La Nuée Bleue, Strasbourg 1994 English translation by Suada Tozo-Waldmann and Carol Mann © Ultrason-Bosnie, Grenoble 1994

Producers: Jean-Jacques Birgé et Corinne Léonet, au nom de tous les participants Associated producers: Catherine Peillon et Benoît Thiebergien, assistant Corinne Julien, for L'Empreinte digitale and Les 38e Rugissants

 

Final editing réalisé by Silvio Soave, Studio GAM, Waimes Sound assistants: René Bensaïd, Emmanuel Conte, Emmanuel Payet and Oliver Jon Pigott Premsastering Si2A

A live version is on YouTube (2h16).
