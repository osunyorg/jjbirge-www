Music for film "Souvenir" directed by Dominique Belloir on painter Jacques Monory , projeté at the entrance of Planétarium of La Cité des Sciences et de l'Industrie, Paris Jean-Jacques Birgé - synthesizer, sampler, vocoder, harmonizer, pocket trumpet, flute, birdcalls Bernard Vitet - trompette Francis Gorgé - E-Bow guitar, synthesizer, melophone, birdcalls Bruno Girard - violin Kent Carter - viola Hélène Bass - cello Marie-Noëlle Sabatelli - cello Geneviève Cabannes - double-bass Mixed on June 17 1986

Yves Prin directs the Ensemble Instrumental du Nouvel Orchestre Philharmonique Jean-Jacques Birgé - synthesizer PPG Bernard Vitet - trumpet Francis Gorgé - electric guitar Unissued original version of Novembre 10 1984 live performance at Grand Auditorium de Radio France - Artistic direction of the recording Michel Bernard - Sound Alain Duchemin) Introduction by Marc Texier A shorter and remixed version features on LP Carnage but we can find this version on the CD reissue (KlangGalerie)

n°6 / Contrefaçons - 1989 / 19'34
