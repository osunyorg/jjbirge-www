Solo of the trumpet player without his trumpet ! Reed trumpet, dragon (balafon type percussion and keyboard of bells), violin, reed... for Mehr Licht !, L'ange du bizarre, Le diable dans le beffroi, La machine de Marly . The first solo album of Vitet (the only real solo of his whole discography) since the mythic La Guêpe on Futura (recently reissued). Interview of Bernard Vitet about Mehr Licht ! (in French) Music : Bernard Vitet Published in 1979 on LP Mehr Licht ! (GRRR 1003) Sound: Daniel Deshays Cover by Vitet from a picture by Isabelle Marmande


n°1 / Mehr Licht ! / 8'43
Bernard Vitet - Bb piccolo trumpet (sopranino saxophone mouthpiece) Recorded in Vitet's staircase, 8 rue Charles Weiss, Paris, in 1979 "Mehr Licht!" are Goethe's last words on his deathbed

n°2 / L'ange du bizarre / 8'43
Bernard Vitet - dragoon (a kind of huge balafon and small bells keyboard) Daniel Deshays  - reportage Recorded in Vitet's studio, 7 rue Charles Weiss, Paris, and in the Puces de Saint-Ouen in 1979 "The Angel of the Odd" is the title of a satirical short story by Edgar Allan Poe

n°3 / Le diable dans le beffroi / 16'00
Bernard Vitet - violin Recorded at Ircam in 1979, in a room which will become later an anechoic chamber and in Saint-Nicolas-du-Chardonnet Church on the day of Marie "The Devil in the Belfry" is the title of a satirical short story by Edgar Allan Poe  

n°4 / La machine de Marly / 7'26
Bernard Vitet - reed instrument made out of a plastic tube with three holes The Blackbird - himself Recorded in 1979 near the railroad in Marly-le-Roi The machine de Marly used to bring the water from the Seine to the Château de Versailles
