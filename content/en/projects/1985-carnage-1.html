A blue cover by painter Jacques Monory. The last LP by Un D.M.I. The trio with guests and a large orchestra. Plus electronics, Amazonian fiction, crazy rock, vocal noises, dirty war, silent phone and a symphonic poem ! Jean-Jacques BIRGÉ (synthesizer, vocals, piano, brass, reeds, percussion) Bernard VITET (trumpets, vocals, violin, reeds) Francis GORGÉ (guitars, synthesizer, vocals, percussion, break) with Jean QUERLIER (oboe, English horn, flute, soprano sax) Youenn LE BERRE (flutes, bassoon) Patrice PETITDIDIER (French horn) Michèle BUIRETTE (accordion) Geneviève CABANNES (double-bass) Youval MICENMACHER (percussion) and Instrumental Ensemble of the NOUVEL ORCHESTRE PHILHARMONIQUE conducted by Yves PRIN Music & words by UN DRAME MUSICAL INSTANTANÉ (Birgé-Vitet-Gorgé) Recorded by Jean-Jacques Birgé Detail of the picture Explosion , 1973 (200x915 cm) by Jacques Monory Portraits by Eric Delva
