n°15 / Jours de cirque - 2002 / 9'07
Audio report recorded at the Grimaldi Forum, Monaco, during the exhibition Jours de cirque ( Circus Days ). The music was located at the cardinal points of the exhibition in order to draw visitors to specific themes. Atmospheric sounds and occasional noises also accompanied certain works. Together, these elements recreated the soundscape of the circus world. Commissaire d’exposition : Zeev Gourrarier Directrice de projet : Sylvie Biancheri Scénographie : Raymond Sarti Assistante scénographe : Sidonie Lenoble Directeur technique : Gérard Nouveau Directeur technique de projet : Marie Agnès D’Anselme Design sonore : Jean Jacques Birgé Musique : Jean-Jacques Birgé et Bernard Vitet Production : Grimaldi Forum. Monaco/ Réunions des Musées Nationaux.

- title: >- n°15 / Jours de cirque - 2002 / 9'07 text: >-

Audio report recorded at the Grimaldi Forum, Monaco, during the exhibition Jours de cirque (external link) (Circus Days).
The music was located at the cardinal points of the exhibition in order to draw visitors to specific themes. Atmospheric sounds and occasional noises also accompanied certain works. Together, these elements recreated the soundscape of the circus world.
Commissaire d’exposition : Zeev Gourrarier
Directrice de projet : Sylvie Biancheri
Scénographie : Raymond Sarti
Assistante scénographe : Sidonie Lenoble
Directeur technique : Gérard Nouveau
Directeur technique de projet : Marie Agnès D’Anselme
Design sonore : Jean Jacques Birgé
Musique : Jean-Jacques Birgé et Bernard Vitet
Production : Grimaldi Forum. Monaco/ Réunions des Musées Nationaux.
