Instantaneous compositions recorded live on October 13, 2024 at Studio GRRR by Jean-Jacques Birgé in the order they were played the audience suggesting titles of books as themes of the improvisations

Hélène Duret – bass clarinet Alexandre Saada – piano Jean-Jacques Birgé - keyboard

n°3 / Agua viva / 13'50
Hélène Duret – bass clarinette, synth-toy, clarinet Alexandre Saada – music boxes, percussion, piano Jean-Jacques Birgé - clavier, percussion

Hélène Duret – clarinet, bass clarinet Alexandre Saada – piano Jean-Jacques Birgé - shahi baaja, Terra, keyboard

Hélène Duret – music boxes, bass clarinet Alexandre Saada – piano Jean-Jacques Birgé - keyboards

Hélène Duret – bass clarinet Alexandre Saada – piano Jean-Jacques Birgé - Enner, keyboards
