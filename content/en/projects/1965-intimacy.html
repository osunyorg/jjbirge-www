n°34 / Envoi à Bernard Vitet, Sud-Ouest #1 - 1977 / 29'28
A few months after the foundation of Un Drame Musical Instantané, and next day after the radio show Un musicien dans la nuit , Jean-Jacques Birgé who is 24 ans drives away for a month to work as audiovisiual technician. From May 22 to 25 1977 he records his thoughts, cuts radiophonies, plays flute and alto sax in the forest... He sends the cassette to Bernard Vitet from Albi.

n°35 / Envoi à Bernard Vitet, Sud-Ouest #2 - 1977 / 52'49
Pentecost week-end. Jean-Jacques Birgé records a second cassette near Lacapelle-Biron in Lot-et-Garonne one week after first one. He sends pieces by Braxton, Puccini's Tosca and Brückner. Before his commentaries on May 21 France Musique redio show one can listen to a radiophone with Radio Moscow. In the woods he practices flute and alto sax.

n°41 / Radio Phony - 2024 / 31'21
On September 22, 2024, to mark her 30th anniversary of radio, sound artist and producer Amandine Casadamont proposed a special program for radio ΠNODE. Sound artists and radio personalities took part in this program, produced live and in public at La Générale. Performances and sonic UFOs followed one another over the airwaves and between the walls, from 5pm to 10pm. After a presentation by Thomas Baumgartner, Jean-Jacques Birgé opened the ball with an improvised piece recalling his youth spent in front of his grandfather Gaston Birgé's huge Telefunken. Jean-Jacques Birgé - radiophonies (plunderphonics), synthesizers, Terra
