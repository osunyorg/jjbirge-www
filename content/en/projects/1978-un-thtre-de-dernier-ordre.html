GRRR 3037 online, 1978

body: >-


Bruno Schnebelin, Tamia, Françoise Achard, Hélène Sage, Gérard Siracusa joined sometimes the trio to become a quartet... 4 improvisations with Françoise Achard among which one live in Paris and another one live in Sens 3h

First piece is on BANDCAMP


n°1 / Un théâtre de dernier ordre - 1978 / 48'54
Jean-Jacques Birgé - synthesizer, vocals, piano, flutes, jaw-harp, melodica, violin, hou-kin, mandoline, Pakistanese banjo, tapes, percussion... Bernard Vitet - trumpet, flugel horn, multiphonic French horn, reeds, vocals, violin, piano, percussion... Francis Gorgé - guitars, bass, violin, percussion... Françoise Achard - vocals, flute, samovar, percussion... Based on a text by Josef von Sternberg Recorded live at Nouveau Carré Sylvia Montfort on April 29 1978

n°2 / 20 000 Leagues Under the Sea - 1978 / 45'28
Jean-Jacques Birgé Bernard Vitet Francis Gorgé Françoise Achard Dedicated to Jules Verne, this piece has been improvised 10 years before CD Sous les mers by Un D.M.I., GRRR 2012 Recorded 7 rue de l'Espérance, Paris 13e, April 7 1978

n°3 / De Max Pallenberg - 1978 / 40'38
Jean-Jacques Birgé Bernard Vitet Francis Gorgé Françoise Achard Recorded 7 rue de l'Espérance, Paris 13e, April 20 1978

n°4 / L'oreille en verre - 1978 / 40'18
Jean-Jacques Birgé - synthesizer ARP 2600, piano, flute, jaw harp, harmonica, trumpets, alto sax, percussion, soundscape Bernard Vitet - trumpet, flugel horn, alto sax, reed trumpet, double bombarde, violin, piano Francis Gorgé - electric guitar and bass, mellophone Françoise Achard - vocals, flute, percussion Sens Music Meeting, live in Sens, September 16 1978
