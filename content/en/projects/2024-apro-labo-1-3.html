Instantaneous compositions recorded live on January 14 2024 at GRRR Studio by Jean-Jacques Birgé in the order which they have been played with the audience participating 56 minutes Also on Bandcamp Cover ©JJB Photo of the trio ©Martin Meissonnier


n°1 / Mentalisme (the protocol) / 2'38
Antonin-Tri Hoang explains the protocol he has devised to spark our imagination. The musical mantalism experiment consists of having someone in the audience listen to one minute of music taken at random from a 5-hour playlist, and asking him or her what it evokes in him or her, avoiding musical terms. The volunteer is the only person to hear it. The musicians will then play his or her explanation.

n°2 / Allumettes Paillasson / 4'23
Mathias Lévy - violin Antonin-Tri Hoang - synthesizer Jean-Jacques Birgé - sampler

n°3 / Particules fines / 6'22
Mathias Lévy - violin Antonin-Tri Hoang - clarinet Jean-Jacques Birgé - shahi baaja

n°4 / Au delà des galaxies / 6'15
Mathias Lévy - violin Antonin-Tri Hoang - synthesizer, percussion Jean-Jacques Birgé - sampler, birdcall, whistling

n°5 / Un gros Sibérien / 7'58
Mathias Lévy - violin Antonin-Tri Hoang - clarinet, synthesizer Jean-Jacques Birgé - sampler, groowah

n°6 / Le train ne s'arrête jamais / 11'49
Mathias Lévy - violon Antonin-Tri Hoang - synthesizer, alto sax, clarinet Jean-Jacques Birgé - Lyra-8, Terra

n°7 / Combat de chiens coréen / 10'32
Mathias Lévy - violin Antonin-Tri Hoang - alto sax Jean-Jacques Birgé - jaw harp, vocals, sampler, balloon

n°8 / Yemen / 6'03
Mathias Lévy - violin Antonin-Tri Hoang - synthesizer, percussion Jean-Jacques Birgé - Enner, sampler
