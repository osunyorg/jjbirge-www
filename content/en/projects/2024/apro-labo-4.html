GRRR 3120 online, 2024

body: >-


Instantaneous compositions recorded live on September 8, 2024 at Studio GRRR by Jean-Jacques Birgé in the order they were played following cards from Oblique Strategies conceived by Brian Eno and Peter Schmidt and randomly chosen by the audience. We have kept the introductions where the audience chooses the cards so as not to lose the spirit of the evening.
